Teaching Resources

Downloadable worksheets, checklists, and templates I’ve created for Food Technology and Industrial Arts.

Food Tech

Year 9 Food Fusion Activity Template

Short worksheet covering macronutrients, label reading, and everyday food examples. Students choose a familiar dish and “fuse” it with another cuisine, sketch an idea, and justify ingredient swaps using the nutrition panel. Quick, one-lesson task with extension prompts and an allergy/sustainability check box.

Food Technology

Year 9 Food Fusion Assessment Task Scaffold

Assessment task scaffold for the Food Fusion unit. It steps students through the brief, research, idea sketches, trial cook, and final product with sections for nutrition analysis, costing, sensory evaluation, and safety. Includes a checklist, success criteria/marking rubric, timeline planner, and sentence starters for the written justification.

Design & Tech

Year 8 Technology, Assessment Port-Folio Template

Scaffold for design brief, research, ideation, prototyping, testing, and evaluation. Year 8 Design & Technology portfolio scaffold that walks students step-by-step from the brief to a final reflection. Includes simple templates (brainstorm, sketches, materials list, safety notes), checkpoints for teacher/peer feedback, and spaces for photos of prototypes and tests. Comes with a mini timeline and success-criteria checklist so students can track progress and quality.

Year 10 Aboriginal Cultural Considerations Mix&Match- Exit Activity

Quick exit ticket to check understanding. Students match short scenarios with the correct cultural consideration (e.g., Acknowledgement of Country, permissions for images/stories, community consultation, respectful language and representation). Takes 5–10 minutes and includes an answer key plus an optional reflection: “How would you change one scenario to be more culturally respectful?”
