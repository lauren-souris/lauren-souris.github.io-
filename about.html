About Me

TAS teacher-in-training with a love for Food Technology, Industrial Arts, and hands-on learning. Currently studying at the Australian Catholic University, designing lessons and building skills that enable students to create, problem-solve, and explore their ideas in safe, supportive environments.

Outside of the classroom, I run a disability support business, working with people of all abilities to help them grow their skills and confidence. Whether it’s in the kitchen, the workshop, or the community, I’m focused on creating equal opportunities for people to learn, connect, and achieve things they once thought impossible.
