My Teaching Philosophy

I strive to create a dynamic classroom where students explore their passions, discover what they love, and build practical skills through hands-on, creative problem-solving.

Core beliefs

  1. Learning is most powerful when it’s meaningful and connected to real contexts.
  2. Student voice matters—choice increases engagement.
  3. High expectations + strong support = growth.
  4. Safety and inclusion underpin every practical activity.
